Rules to classify the Customers

High Risk
- If there are more than 10 incoming ot outgoing transaction in a month from high risk country
- If sum of incoming transaction is more than 1000$ in a month
- If sum of outgoing transaction is more than 800$ in a month
- If number of transactions is more than 20 in a day
Medium Risk
- If there are more than 10 incoming ot outgoing transaction in a month from high risk/Non -high country
- If sum of incoming transaction is more than 600$ and less than $1000 in a month
- If sum of outgoing transaction is more than 500$ and less than $80in a month
- If number of transactions is more than 10 but less than 20 in a day
Low Risk
- If there are more than 10 incoming ot outgoing transaction in a month from non-high risk country
- If sum of incoming transaction is less than 6000$ in a month
- If sum of outgoing transaction is less than 500$ in a month
- If number of transactions is less than 10 in a day
